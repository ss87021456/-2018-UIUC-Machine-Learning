Recall a hard SVM is as follows:
\begin{align}\label{eq:svm}
\min\limits_{w, b} \frac{1}{2} \norm{\bw}^2 \;\;\;
\text{s.t.} \;\; y^{(i)}(\bw^\intercal\bx^{(i)} + b) \geq 1 \;\; ,\forall (x^{(i)}, y^{(i)}) \in \cD
\end{align}
